Sanai School System

Sanai School System aim to develop a truly inclusive, successful and diverse academic skills set with a global perspective for your child. Being internationally recognized and accredited, we coach and counsel, providing the chance to benefit from the diversity of languages offered and to enhance skill development through award-winning digital literacy and ICT integration programmes. Being internationally recognized and accredited, we coach and counsel, providing the chance to benefit from the diversity of languages offered and to enhance skill development through award-winning digital literacy and ICT integration programmes.


Our Mission

The Sanai School System is committed in its pursuit of excellence, by imparting the quality education with the best academic facilities and atmosphere to its students. Our mission is to train future leaders and professionals who set new standards in knowledge, caring and compassion. Its exceptionally well qualified and committed faculty provides combination of nurturing support and challenge to the students to reach their maximum potential. We shall continue to transform the contents, methodology of teaching and technological resources according to dictates of time.

Our Vision

Ensure that all students are happy throughout their time at the school and graduate feeling of enthusiasism about life. Develop new friendships and gain a sense of achievement in an ethical, highly productive and motivated environment. Deliver at primary education level a formal, broad education focusing on literacy and numeracy as well as introducing children to the Study Technology. Deliver an education to children whose prior school experience or progress may not have been adequate and permit such students to flourish and achieve results to the level of their potential.
